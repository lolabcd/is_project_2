How to Download Your CSV Files

Follow these simple steps to download your social media data as CSV files:

  1. Go to the Meta Ads Manager.
  2. Log in using your credentials.
  3. Navigate to the side bar on the left and click on 'insights'.
  4. Navigate to the "Results" section, where you see different insight options
  5. Choose the report insights you want to export.
  6. Click on the "Export" button and select "CSV" as the format.
  7. Your CSV file will be downloaded to your device.
  8. Click on the "UPLOAD"button below and upload your .csv file
Tip: Download all insight types to ensure a comprehensive report
